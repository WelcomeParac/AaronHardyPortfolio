Project Details / Background
Problem: Creating a rally magazine publication that puts emphasis on the cars and their history without information overload.
Solution: In crafting the magazine's layout, I aimed for a straightforward and clutter-free design to effectively emphasize the rally cars and their glory. My focus on simplicity and clear communication was evident through the use of clean typography, a limited color palette, and generous white space. The result was an asethetically pleasing layout that enabled seamless content navigation, emphasizing the vital role of design in delivering the history of those rally cars, ensuring the readers can engage in the text without distractions or information overload.
Programs: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Indesign, Adobe Illustrator
